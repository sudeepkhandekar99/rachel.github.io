Silent/Siren Prayer

acrylic & watercolor paints on paper

Back when I was in school - an ambulance siren would easily catch my attention. My heart would beat faster while my eyes traced the speeding vehicle. Every time a siren screeched, she would bow her head - as if on cue.
"Why?"
"A prayer for their good health ..."
Lately, I've been wondering whether she gets a chance to utter "amen" - before the next siren screeches.

acrylic paints on paper

The Jungle by Upton Sinclair gives an account of the old meatpacking industry in Chicago. It also talks about the immigrants who chased the American Dream and their desperate attempts to merely live when the dream seemed nothing more than a mirage. This heart-wrenching story portrays the cost of capitalism on people. However, it had an impact different from what the author expected or hoped to make.
