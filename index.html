Silent/Siren Prayer

acrylic & watercolor paints on paper

Back when I was in school - an ambulance siren would easily catch my attention. My heart would beat faster while my eyes traced the speeding vehicle. Every time a siren screeched, she would bow her head - as if on cue.
"Why?"
"A prayer for their good health ..."
Lately, I've been wondering whether she gets a chance to utter "amen" - before the next siren screeches.

The Daily Moments

It was around the peak of COVID and lockdown anxieties when this project randomly came into form on a Sunday evening. My friend shared an Instagram post which led me to ask people to share their happy moments. I chose to design the compilation as a newspaper to give everyone a relaxing read to complement their evening tea.
